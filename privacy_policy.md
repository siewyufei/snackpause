# Privacy Policy for Snack Pause

**Effective date:** July 8, 2026

Snack Pause is designed to help users pause before eating, reflect on cravings and emotions, try self-soothing activities, view local history and statistics, and export their own records.

This Privacy Policy explains how Snack Pause handles information.

## Summary

Snack Pause does not require an account. We do not sell personal data. We do not show ads. We do not use third-party advertising trackers. The app is designed to store your craving records locally on your device.

## Information You Enter

You may choose to enter:

- The food you are thinking about eating
- Whether you use Quick mode or Full mode
- Stress level
- Physical hunger level
- Loneliness level
- Boredom level
- Sadness level
- Snack urge level
- Emotion intensity in Quick mode
- Self-soothing activity preferences
- Your reminder result and local history records

This information is used only to provide app features, such as local reminders, pause need estimates, supportive snack ideas, self-soothing activity suggestions, Pause timer support, history, statistics, text-to-speech playback, and CSV export.

## Local Storage

Your records are stored locally on your device. Snack Pause does not operate a user account system or a cloud database for your records.

If you delete the app, your local app data may be deleted by iOS.

## CSV Export

Snack Pause lets you export selected local history as a CSV file. When you export a CSV file, you choose where to save or share it using Apple's system sharing and file tools. After export, the privacy and security of that file depends on where you save it or who you share it with.

## Text-to-Speech

Snack Pause may read reminder text aloud using Apple's speech features on your device. The app does not use this feature to record your voice.

## App Store and Apple Services

Apple may process information related to downloading, installing, updating, crash reporting, analytics, or device-level services according to Apple's own policies and your device settings. Snack Pause does not control Apple's handling of that information.

## Data We Do Not Collect

Snack Pause does not intentionally collect or transmit:

- Your name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Precise location
- Contacts
- Photos or videos
- Voice recordings
- Advertising identifiers
- Payment information

## Tracking and Advertising

Snack Pause does not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies. Snack Pause does not use your data for third-party advertising.

## Health and Wellness Disclaimer

Snack Pause is a self-reflection and habit-support tool. It is not medical advice, nutritional advice, mental health treatment, diagnosis, therapy, or professional counseling. The app is not intended to treat eating disorders or any medical or mental health condition. If you have concerns about eating behavior, mental health, nutrition, or physical health, please seek advice from a qualified professional.

## Children

Snack Pause is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use digital services without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect children's personal data.

## Security

Snack Pause relies on Apple's iOS app sandboxing, device security, and local storage protections. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, so you should protect your device with a passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, or other appropriate safeguards.

## Your Choices

You can:

- Choose not to enter a food name or scores
- Delete app history from within the app where available
- Export your own CSV records
- Delete the app to remove local app data from your device, subject to iOS behavior and backups

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date and, where appropriate, provide notice in the app or App Store listing.
